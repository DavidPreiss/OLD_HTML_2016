What do engineers do?

Engineers use math and science to solve practical problems. While most other white collar proffessions, and indeed, most jobs in general, deal only with maintaining society, engineers work to improve it. In general, Engineers bring the abstract ideas of others into the real world.

In construction, the architect may create the plans for the building, but it must then be approved by an engineer to ensure that it is actually possible to build within the given constraints. Since engineering is required in order to realize most worthwhile pursuits, there are many different fields of specialization. Here is a short list of just a few of the careers within engineering.

  1. Chemical Engineering
  2. Mechanical Engineering
  3. Material Engineering
  4. Electrical Engineering
  5. Industrial Engineering
  6. Civil Engineering
  7. Computer Engineering

Computer engineering

Computer engineers research, design, develop, and test computer systems and components such as processors, circuit boards, memory devices, networks, and routers. Circuitry architecture is very important.

  1. Hardware Engineer
  2. Software Engineer
  3. Robotics Engineer
  4. Game developer
  5. Website designer
