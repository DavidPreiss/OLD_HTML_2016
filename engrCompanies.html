What types of companies hire engineers?

  1. Intel

    Intel is a multinational American technology company known for selling some of, if not the best, microprocessors in the world. Salaries for software engineers go from 58 to 140 thousand dollars a year.

    Hours and work environment vary, although one common report is a satisfaction of working on the leading edge of technology. But while some report it as a dream job, stories come out of highly educated professional engineers and programmers ground down by a highly antiquated corporate environment, crushed by 60+ hour weeks and corrupt management. Intel has locations in 19 states and Washington D.C. with a lot of them residing in California.

    Types of Products
    • microprocessors
    • Solid state drives
    • Bluetooth chipsets

    Services Offered
    • Tech support
    • Cloud Storage
  2. Apple

    Apple is also a multinational American technology company located in California. However it sells consumer electronics and actually buys microprocessors from Intel to use is their devices. Apple has 478 retail stores across 17 countries.

    Eerily similar to Intel, the number 1 most commonly claimed perk of working at Apple was the feeling that the work done mattered and significantly impacted the world. However rumors and reports mention a work environment just as oppressive as Intel, but with issues more focued on toxicity and sexism. This probably less of a problem with Intel because women make up barely more than 10% of their workers, while Apple rocks a whole 32%. Hardware engineers at Apple make between 85 and 149 thousand a year, with some Senior software engineers making over 190 thousad per year.

    Types of Products
    • iPhone smartphone
    • iPad tablet computer
    • iPod portable media player
    • Apple Watch smartwatch

    Services Offered
    • Apple Tech support
    • iCloud Storage
    • iMessage
    • iTunes Licensed Music
